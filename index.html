About

A Workshop Built Around Vintage Suzuki TS Motorcycles

Two Stroke Works is rooted in a simple idea: bring classic Suzuki TS bikes back to life with care, patience, and mechanical honesty. From full restorations to parts, details matter.

Whether it is a clean rider, a period-correct rebuild, or a hard-to-find component, the goal is always the same — preserve the look, sound, and feel that made these bikes memorable in the first place.
